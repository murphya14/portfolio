Return to work


This is a short course created with Adobe Captivate and Inkscape.

The purpose of this course is to provide an informative and re-assuring introduction to how a company plans on returning to work.

To engage the user, I incorprated vivid graphics and began with setting a brief foundation for the virus (clear definition.)


In assessing the needs of the learner, I would like to:
  • set a base understanding of the signs and symptons.
  • make them aware of what measures the company is taking.
  • asking for them to take active and individual respondsibilty and how this can be achieved.
  • show the learner how their efforts have been appreciated over the pandemic.
